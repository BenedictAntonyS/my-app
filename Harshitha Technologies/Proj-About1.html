Who we are

Harshitha Technologies incorporated in the year 2011.
An IT solution Provider offering IT Solutions sales and services across verticals.
An organization synonymous with the best practices in IT Solutions, sales and services of a complete range of IT products.
We are driven by strong work ethics, passion for technology, and an eternal Quest for improving our knowledge base. We are catering to the software and hardware requirements of all the segments of the industry by providing more value to the services we provide. We offer the entire range of software products starting from desktop products, application software, and web-based development tools, security and backup solutions too.
The services we offer range from pre-sales consulting, domain-specific consulting, deployment, systems integration, custom software development, Managed Services, Network design / Management.
To service our customers better, we have aligned with some of the global leaders in the IT industry including Microsoft, Kaspersky, Symantec, Mcafee, Adobe, Oracle, Dell, Lenovo, IBM, HP, AMD, Acer, Fujitsu, etc.
In short, we are fulfilling all the IT requirements for various industries and business houses with cost-effective and excellent service attached to it. Our strategic tie-up with the leading vendors of the IT industry enables us to provide customers a wide range of computing products at the right price each time to match the expectations and standards.
Our Endeavour is not only the sale of products to our valued clients but also to render after-sales services as and when required.
